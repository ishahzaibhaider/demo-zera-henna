Quick answers

How dark does the stain get?

Dark. It’s the house promise — organic paste stains deepest 24–48 hours after the sitting and holds for one to two weeks with basic aftercare (which Sahdha sends you on WhatsApp).

Do you travel to my emirate?

Yes — based in Sharjah, home service across all seven emirates. Travel is quoted with your booking so there are no surprises on the day.

How early should brides book?

Two to four weeks ahead is comfortable. Eid weeks and peak wedding season go first — if your date is fixed, message today and hold it.

Can I order cones without an appointment?

Absolutely. ZH organic cones are hand-rolled in the studio and delivered anywhere in the UAE — same paste Sahdha uses on brides.
